About

Elissa Aron is an actress and singer, and is currently living in New York City.

Although she calls the Sunshine State her hometown, Elissa has been raised all over the United States, and has lived abroad several times. Being the daughter of a cellist, Elissa grew up understanding the importance of music and uses music as a foundation in her work. She also has a sharp sense of humor which she developed growing up the middle child in a very loud family, where she used her wit to get attention and to construct lots of quick comebacks.

Now a college graduate, Elissa still loves getting attention! Elissa graduated from NYU in 2012, where she studied musical theater at CAP21 and screen acting at Stonestreet Studios. She loves everything New York City has to offer, although she would be thankful for sunnier weather!
